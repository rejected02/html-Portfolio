Summary

"Lost and Found" is an immersive puzzle-adventure game where players take on the role of a detective tasked with finding lost objects in a mysterious environment. Players interact with a dynamically changing table, where objects appear and disappear based on in-game events. Each object holds a clue, leading the player deeper into the storyâ€™s unfolding mystery.

Utilizing Unity, the game incorporates real-time object instantiation and data management, ensuring an interactive and seamless gameplay experience. Advanced physics-based mechanics, such as a dynamic chain system connecting two characters, add realism and challenge to movement dynamics. The AI-driven follower car maintains a strategic distance from the leader, adding depth to vehicular pursuits.

On the web development side, the game integrates an online progress tracker and community-driven leaderboards, built using modern web technologies. A responsive and engaging UI ensures that players stay connected to their progress across multiple devices. With engaging puzzles, intuitive mechanics, and smooth physics interactions, Lost and Found delivers an engaging and rewarding experience for players who love unraveling mysteries.
